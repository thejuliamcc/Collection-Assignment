Collection Clowns Bunnies

The first time I saw clowns I wanted was in Niederbusch. They were called Gildeclowns. It was 2011. I bought three.

None of these pictures are my Gildeclowns. The top 3 were given to me by my mother-in-law, the second set of 3 I’ve forgotten. The bottom row was a gift from my aunt. There’s also a Jack Skellington in there because it was Halloween.

These two are recent acquisitions from Prudhommes Antique Market. The previous owner of the larger clown seemed pretty adamant that I buy it. The smaller fellow I found a few weeks ago at another Prudhommes stall. The similiar colour scheme was what drew me to it.

Neither of them have labels to say where they were made.

The rest of my clowns are in storage.
