Collection Clowns Bunnies

I started to collect rabbit paraphernalia before I owned a bunny. I got my first real bunny in 2015. His name is Ragnar.

Although it may seem so, I don’t collect indiscriminately. These bunny plates, for example, I did not purchase. Although they’re adorable (and maybe it’s gross sentimentalism at work), there is a certain weirdness missing.

I saw them at Craftworks outside of Peterborough. Maybe I’ll change my mind and go get them.

These two buns were also acquired at Prudhommes, off the QEW. Here they are in their natural habitat, but I did take them home shortly afterward.

I enjoy seasonal decorations, which are ripe grounds for tackiness. I pull out my bunny decor when the snow first melts. I also have pastel-toned clowns that I call my Spring Clowns.
